About Sport

What Is Sport?

Sport is a form of physical activity or game. Often competitive and organized, sports use, maintain, or improve physical ability and skills.

Historical Sport

Artifacts suggest sports in China as early as 2000 BC. Monuments in Egypt indicate regulated sports several thousands of years ago.
